Recipe 1 - Pancakes

Pancakes - my favorite desert

List of ingredients:

  • flour
  • baking powder
  • sugar
  • salt
  • milk
  • butter
  • egg

Steps:

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together.
  2. Make a well, then add the wet ingredients. Stir to combine.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle or pan.
  4. Cook for two to three minutes, then flip.
  5. Continue cooking until brown on both sides.

Recipe 2 - Brownies

This is an easy recipe for begginers in baking

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • water
  • powdered sugar
  • cocoa powder
  • oil
  • vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. First, mix together the dry and wet ingredients in two separate bowls.
  2. Combine the sugar, flour, powdered sugar, cocoa powder, chocolate chips, and salt in a medium bowl.
  3. Then, whisk together the eggs, olive oil, and water in a large one.
  4. Next, combine the wet and dry ingredients.
  5. Then, pour the batter into an 8X8 inch baking pan lined with parchment paper.
  6. Transfer the pan to a 325-degree oven and bake for 40 to 45 minutes.

Recipe 3 - Cinnamon rolls

Only for cinnamon lovers

Ingredients:

  • milk
  • sugar
  • yeast
  • butter
  • egg+egg yolk
  • flour
  • cinnamon
  • salt
  • vanilla

Steps:

  1. Warm milk to around 110 degrees F. I like to do this by placing milk in a microwave safe bowl and microwaving it for 40-45 seconds.
  2. Transfer warm milk to the bowl of an electric mixer and sprinkle yeast on top. Add in sugar, egg, egg yolk and melted butter. Mix until well combined.
  3. Next stir in flour and salt with a wooden spoon until a dough begins to form.
  4. Place dough hook on stand mixer and knead dough on medium speed for 8 minutes. Dough should form into a nice ball and be slightly sticky
  5. Transfer dough ball to a well-oiled bowl, cover with plastic wrap and a warm towel. Allow dough to rise for 1 hour to 1 ½ hours, or until doubled in size.
  6. Spread softened butter over dough, leaving a ¼ inch margin at the far side of the dough.
  7. Tightly roll dough up, starting from the 9-inch side and place seam side down making sure to seal the edges of the dough as best you can.
  8. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Remove plastic wrap and towel and bake cinnamon rolls for 20-25 minutes or until just slightly golden brown on the edges.
